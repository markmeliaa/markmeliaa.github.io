AN ALL-TIME CLASSIC GAME

Minesweeper is a game we all know and love, so for one of my very first own projects as a programmer, I decided to recreate one in Python, making use of everything that was learned during the first year of the University degree.

Also, to give it a bit of a twist, I decided to implement three different difficulty levels, so the most skilled players could find it challenging!
