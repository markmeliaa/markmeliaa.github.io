A JOURNEY THROUGH THE STARS

The icing on the cake of my University degree, a rogue-lite game in which the player controls Ophiuchus, the newest zodiac sign, and who will have to discover what the stars are hiding from him, and all of this following a procedural narrative that will have a unique evolution for each player.

Nowadays, this is just a show-off demo, as the complete narrative is not yet included, but I will be updating it periodically if I make any advances or changes.
