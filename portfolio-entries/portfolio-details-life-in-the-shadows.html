ESCAPE AND DEFEAT ENEMIES

This game was born from a university assignment, but I really liked the idea of making my first game for NDS/GBA, so I decided to do as much as I could and this was the final result, a game where you are trapped inside a dungeon with barely any light and you have to escape, defeat monsters and save your friend.

Either the NDS cartridge and the GBA cartridge are in the GitHub repository, the only thing needed to play is a NDS/GBA emulator, and as a curiosity, the art style is done tile by tile inside the .cpp file as it was compulsory to draw the tiles there and then draw a whole map with them.
