A JOURNEY TO SAVE YOUR FRIEND

This was my really first videogame, a basic dungeon game in which the player has to go around searching some items, opening some doors and killing some enemies in order to reach the objective, rescue the friend that has been trapped inside.

It can be played by any emulator (I used WinDS PRO), and with this game I not only wanted to discover how games for NDS and GBA are developed, but also try to do anything new outside the languages we were seeing in the degree (C#, Python, C++...).
