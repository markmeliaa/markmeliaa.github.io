DAILY STATS FOR COVID-19

Due to the pandemic and lockdown that happened some years ago, several of our projects in the University were Covid-related for a while, and one of those projects was this Android application, which keeps track of daily information about Covid cases and more.

This informative app was developed in Kotlin and it uses an API from the internet that was provided to us; it reads and interprets its data and then displays the results of each requested day in cronological order. And it not only shows the cases that were each day, but also other important information such as the recovered or the deaths.
